LE COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE SERVICES

LE Commercial Real Estate Services is a full-service commercial real estate brokerage firm based in Houston, Texas, we are in the business of servicing commercial property owners in income-producing commercial real estate properties throughout the state of Texas. Our professional commercial real estate team is able to bring their in-depth knowledge and expert advice in investment sales, leasing, and property management in commercial real estate for any investor’s specific needs. Our clients have direct access to the marketplace, key relationships, and the latest market movements that is a necessity to any owner of commercial properties in the market today. Our track record and results-oriented focus has been the key to our client’s real estate success in the Texas commercial real estate market. Our company specializes in maintaining top property and portfolio value through the best lease structure and negotiations, keeping expenses low for our owners and tenants, and expert advice on how to be proactive instead of reacting to marketing movement. These company values have made our commercial real estate firm one of Houston’s premier commercial real estate brokerage firm. Our strategic partnership with our clients gives them the competitive advantage that is a necessity in today's ever-changing real estate market place.

Our team members have over 20 years of commercial real estate experience in investing, commercial real estate sales, leasing, and property management services in Houston, Texas. Our managing partners have a track-record of selling in excess of $100,000,000 in real estate over the last decade, and have been able to assist our clients to maintain top property value for their entire commercial real estate portfolio. Any commercial property owner can feel confident that their asset disposition needs will be met with a fast and smooth investment sale process to achieve top market price. Our real estate owners have benefited from our retailer and vendor relationships, market knowledge and experiences, and our cutting-edge technology and tools
