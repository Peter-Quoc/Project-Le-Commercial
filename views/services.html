Advisory Services

LE Commercial Real Estate Services provides a wide array of real estate advisory and consultation services to all types of investors with various needs. Our advisors know that each investor has different objectives and portfolio strategies, from addressing property operating issues, an aggressive leasing plan aimed to increase occupancy in a high vacancy submarket, to disposing and selling of a distress commercial building for lending institution. We pride ourselves on having a solution for any type of client that might request our services. We offer commercial real estate consultation with the property owner’s key objectives in mind, and that is specific to the asset, property portfolio, with the property’s specific submarket strengths and weakness in consideration.

  • Broker Opinion of Value
  • Asset Valuation and Analysis
  • Buy-Sell-Hold Strategy Consultation
  • Investment Portfolio and Strategy
  • Market Positioning and Submarket Analysis
  • Development and Redevelopment Feasibility Studies
  • Property NNN and Expense Reduction Analysis

Investment Sales

LE Commercial Real Estate investment Sales Division is committed to providing our exclusive listings to sell with the broadest exposure to the most active commercial real estate buyers in the market place. Our focus is to implement a specific marketing plan for each of our commercial sales listing, so that the property listing will receive national market exposure to active buyers, as well as international capital looking to place money into U.S. real estate assets. We understand that selling commercial real estate properties in today’s marketplace requires more than an internet posting and waiting for a buyer to call, we believe a brokerage firm should be proactive in creating a market for any property.
